Aim

The aim of this workshop is to promote collaboration and knowledge exchange in the field of Systems, Man, and Cybernetics across Asia, with a particular emphasis on building a strong research network and fostering the development of young and student researchers. This workshop consists of invited talks by leading researchers in the field and general sessions emphasizing discussion and interaction.

本ワークショップは，アジア地域におけるSystems, Man, Cybernetics分野での協力と知識交流を推進し，研究ネットワークの強化と学生を含む若手研究者の育成を目的としている．ワークショップは，当該分野の第一線で活躍する研究者による招待講演と，活発な議論と意見交換を重視した一般講演で構成する．なお，一般講演に関しては，日本語での研究発表も受け付ける．

Abstract Submission

We use the Microsoft CMT for the abstract submission. Please access to https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/SMCJW2026.

Each abstract submission should include the background, research purpose, approach, contributions, and future work. Please also choose the subject areas that describe your research well. If you cannot find an appropriate keyword in the subject area list, you can also write your keyword down.
If you have some related articles, you can upload up to 3 files. Those files will be used only for the internal evaluation process. This is just an option, not mandatory.

The Microsoft CMT service was used for managing the peer-reviewing process for this conference. This service was provided for free by Microsoft and they bore all expenses, including costs for Azure cloud services as well as for software development and support.

Venue

The conference venue is the Rinku Campus of Osaka Metropolitan University. It is very near to Kansai International Airport. The nearest station is Rinku Town station (JR Line and Nankai Line).

Izumisano City is located almost midway between Osaka City and Wakayama City and is blessed with rich natural surroundings. The city has developed in a well-balanced way, encompassing commerce, industry, agriculture, and fishing. Please visit the webpage to explore Izumisano City.
