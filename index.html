Aim

The aim of this workshop is to promote collaboration and knowledge exchange in the field of Systems, Man, and Cybernetics across Asia, with a particular emphasis on building a strong research network and fostering the development of young and student researchers. This workshop consists of invited talks by leading researchers in the field and general sessions emphasizing discussion and interaction.

本ワークショップは，アジア地域におけるSystems, Man, Cybernetics分野での協力と知識交流を推進し，研究ネットワークの強化と学生を含む若手研究者の育成を目的としている．ワークショップは，当該分野の第一線で活躍する研究者による招待講演と，活発な議論と意見交換を重視した一般講演で構成する．なお，一般講演に関しては，日本語での研究発表も受け付ける．

Topics

Agentic AI, Artificial Life, Assistive Technology, Biometric and Brain-Based Systems, Cognitive Systems, Complex Systems, Cybernetics, Decision Support Systems, Deep and Representation Learning, Enterprise and Industrial Information Systems, Ethics and Trustworthy AI, Evolutionary Computation, Fault Diagnosis and Resilience Engineering, Fuzzy Systems, Human-Centered Systems, Human-Machine Cooperation, Image and Pattern Recognition, Information Visualization, Intelligent Control, Intelligent Green Systems, Intelligent Transportation Systems, Intelligent Vehicles, Interactive Media, IoT and Smart Sensor, Kansei Engineering, Medical and Health Informatics, Quantum Computing, Robotics, Service Systems, Smart Cities and Infrastructure, Swarm Intelligence, Virtual and Augmented Reality Systems
